Privacy Policy

Effective: July 17, 2026

Overview

Nation Eater does not create a separate developer account. The core game can be played offline without signing in or viewing ads.

The Operations panel contains only an optional rewarded ad chosen by the player. There are no banners, automatic interstitials, or app-open ads.

Optional Rewarded Advertising

Optional rewarded ads are capped at five per local day. Rewards are 5 Public Gems, 5 Public Gems, 15 minutes of passive funds, one free upgrade, and 30 minutes of passive funds in that order. Watching ads is never required to obtain core progression, characters, tools, or endings.

Google Mobile Ads SDK and User Messaging Platform (UMP) may process an IP address, approximate location, device identifiers, advertising data, ad and video interactions, crash logs, performance data, and diagnostics for ad delivery, measurement, consent, and fraud prevention.

Ad requests are non-personalized. The app does not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission or condition a reward on tracking consent. Where UMP requires it, choices can be changed through Ad Privacy Choices in Settings.

The developer does not directly collect ad events on a separate developer-operated server or sell personal data. Google's handling is governed by Google's privacy policy and the player's consent choices.

Remove Ads In-App Purchase

Remove Ads is a non-consumable Apple App Store in-app purchase. StoreKit verifies the entitlement; neither the app nor the developer receives payment-card details.

Owners automatically receive the same five-reward package once per active day, with no backlog for missed days, and can restore the purchase from the Operations panel.

Information Stored on the Device

Game progress, collection records, audio, haptic, language, motion settings, and diagnostics are stored on the current device. Diagnostics remain on-device until the player manually copies or shares them with the developer.

Optional Game Center/iCloud Sync

When the player enables automatic sync and Game Center and iCloud are available, game progress may be stored by Apple as Gameplay Content linked to the player's Apple gaming account.

Sync is used only for app functionality. There is no separate developer-operated server, and the developer does not receive or store synced saves. Apple's policies and account settings govern Apple's handling of this data.

Retention and Deletion

Local data remains until the player resets it in Settings or deletes the app. Automatic sync can be disabled at any time, and the iCloud save can be deleted through a two-confirmation action in Settings.

Deleting the app alone may not delete a cloud save. Settings also provides save export, import, conflict candidate selection, and pre-import backup restoration.

Contact

For privacy or save-deletion questions, contact the address published on the public support page.
